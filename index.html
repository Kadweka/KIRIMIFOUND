Hi Guys!

Kirimi Foundation it's a registered organization under NGO Board,it was formed on 1st Jan 2020 and later launched on 28th June 2020,it was founded by Martin Kirimi who's the CEO currently, basically we do charity programs,our main objective is to fight poverty and restoring dignity in the community.

What We Do

Feed Street Families

Alot of Families have been hit by Covid19 and as our always desire we cook food and help the hungry people in streets.Previously we visited Jerusha Mwilaria Hope Children's home and donated foodstuffs,clothes, soaps,snacks

Feed The Elderly

As the we usualy Do we help also the Elderly In Different ways but mostly we provide to them food and items for them to use while at home.Alot of families are having a really really hard time and for this it boosts them either

Give Sanitory Towels

Over 5k towels,facemasks and sanitizers are Donated in Kooje,Shauri yako,Majengo and makutano.We also donated facemasks and sanitary towels to Bishop Lawi mathiu Sec school,Consolata,Willy`s Academy,Kinoru Day school

Giving Scholarship

Out there we have very bright students who are hardworking and have excellent perfomance.We support these By launching a Scholarship Programme to help these kind of Students and to give them Hope
